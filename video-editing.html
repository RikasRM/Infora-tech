Frequently Asked Questions

How long does video editing take?

Video editing timelines vary based on project complexity. Simple edits (1-2 minutes) typically take 1-2 days, while complex projects with motion graphics and effects may take 1-2 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during project planning and offer rush services for urgent projects at additional cost.

What video formats do you work with?

We work with all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, and RAW formats from professional cameras. We can also handle various audio formats and provide final deliverables in multiple formats optimized for different platforms (web, social media, broadcast, etc.).

How much do video editing services cost in Sri Lanka?

Video editing costs depend on project complexity, duration, and requirements. Basic editing starts from LKR 15,000, while complex projects with motion graphics and effects range from LKR 25,000-75,000. We provide detailed quotes based on your specific needs and offer package deals for multiple videos.

Do you provide video content creation from scratch?

Yes, we offer complete video content creation services including concept development, scriptwriting, storyboarding, filming (if required), editing, and post-production. We can create videos from scratch or work with your existing footage to produce professional-quality content.

What software do you use for video editing?

We use industry-standard software including Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing, Adobe After Effects for motion graphics and visual effects, Adobe Audition for audio editing, and DaVinci Resolve for color grading. This ensures professional-quality results and compatibility with various platforms.

Do you provide revisions and feedback?

Yes, we include 2-3 rounds of revisions in our standard packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product. We welcome your feedback and work closely with you throughout the editing process to create videos that meet your exact requirements and vision.
