Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy that typically shows initial results within 3-6 months. However, significant improvements in rankings and traffic usually occur within 6-12 months. Factors affecting timeline include website age, competition, content quality, and technical optimization. We provide monthly reports to track progress.

What's the difference between SEO and SEM?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on improving organic (free) search rankings through technical optimization, content creation, and link building. SEM (Search Engine Marketing) includes both organic SEO and paid advertising like Google Ads. SEM provides immediate results through paid ads while SEO builds long-term organic visibility.

How much do SEO services cost in Sri Lanka?

SEO service costs vary based on scope and competition. Basic SEO packages start from LKR 25,000/month, while comprehensive SEO & SEM packages range from LKR 50,000-150,000/month. We offer customized packages based on your business goals, target keywords, and competition level.

Do you guarantee first-page rankings?

While we cannot guarantee specific rankings due to search engine algorithm changes and competition, we guarantee improvement in your website's search visibility, organic traffic, and overall SEO performance. We use proven strategies and provide transparent reporting to show measurable results.

What keywords should I target for my business?

We conduct comprehensive keyword research to identify the best keywords for your business based on search volume, competition level, and conversion potential. We target a mix of high-volume broad keywords and long-tail keywords that are more likely to convert visitors into customers.

Do you provide monthly SEO reports?

Yes, we provide detailed monthly SEO reports that include keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, conversion rates, technical SEO metrics, competitor analysis, and recommendations for improvement. Our reports help you understand the ROI of your SEO investment and track progress toward your goals.
