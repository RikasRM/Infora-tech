Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a complete brand identity package?

A complete brand identity package typically includes: Primary and secondary logo designs, color palette with hex codes, typography selection, brand guidelines document, business card design, letterhead design, and basic marketing materials. We can customize packages based on your specific needs.

How long does it take to design a logo?

Logo design typically takes 1-2 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes research, concept development, client feedback, and revisions. Complex branding projects may take 3-4 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide source files with the designs?

Yes, we provide all source files including Adobe Illustrator (.ai), Photoshop (.psd), and vector formats (.svg, .eps). We also deliver web-ready formats (.png, .jpg) and print-ready files (.pdf) to ensure you have everything you need for various applications.

Can you design for both digital and print media?

Absolutely! We design for both digital and print media with proper color management and file formats. We ensure designs look great on screens and in print, using appropriate color profiles (RGB for digital, CMYK for print) and resolution settings.

How many revisions are included in the design process?

We include 3 rounds of revisions in our standard design packages. This allows for concept refinement and ensures you're completely satisfied with the final design. Additional revisions can be added if needed, and we're committed to delivering designs that exceed your expectations.

Do you provide ongoing design support?

Yes, we offer ongoing design support and maintenance packages. This includes creating new marketing materials, updating existing designs, seasonal campaigns, and ensuring brand consistency across all your visual communications.
