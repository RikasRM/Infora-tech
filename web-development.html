Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a website?

Website development timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom web applications may take 6-12 weeks. Factors include features, design complexity, content requirements, and functionality. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase.

What's the difference between web design and web development?

Web design focuses on the visual appearance and user experience (UI/UX), including layout, colors, typography, and user interface elements. Web development involves the technical implementation, including coding, database integration, functionality, and server-side programming. We provide both services for complete website solutions.

How much do web development services cost in Sri Lanka?

Web development costs depend on project scope and complexity. Basic websites start from LKR 50,000, while complex e-commerce sites range from LKR 150,000-500,000. Custom web applications can cost LKR 300,000-1,000,000+. We provide detailed quotes based on your specific requirements and offer flexible payment plans.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive hosting and domain services including domain registration, web hosting, SSL certificates, email hosting, and website maintenance. We offer reliable hosting solutions with 99.9% uptime, regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support to ensure your website runs smoothly.

What technologies do you use for web development?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js for frontend; Node.js, Python, PHP for backend; MySQL, MongoDB for databases; and cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and scalability needs.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive ongoing support including website updates, security patches, performance optimization, content updates, technical support, and regular maintenance. We provide different support packages to suit your needs and ensure your website remains secure, fast, and up-to-date.
