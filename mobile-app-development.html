Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

Development time varies based on complexity. Simple apps typically take 2-3 months, while complex applications may take 4-8 months. Factors include features, platforms (iOS/Android/both), design complexity, and integration requirements. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Should I develop for iOS, Android, or both platforms?

We recommend cross-platform development for most businesses as it reaches both iOS and Android users with a single codebase, reducing development time and cost. However, if you have specific platform requirements or need native performance, we can develop platform-specific apps.

What is the cost of mobile app development?

App development costs vary based on features, complexity, platforms, and design requirements. Simple apps start from LKR 500,000, while complex enterprise apps can cost LKR 2,000,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements and can offer flexible payment plans.

Do you provide app store submission and publishing services?

Yes, we handle the complete app store submission process including app store optimization, metadata preparation, screenshot creation, and publishing to both Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We also provide ongoing ASO services to improve your app's visibility.

What technologies do you use for mobile app development?

We use modern technologies including React Native, Flutter, Swift (iOS), Kotlin (Android), and native development tools. We choose the best technology stack based on your requirements, ensuring optimal performance, scalability, and maintainability for your app.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive ongoing support including bug fixes, feature updates, performance optimization, security updates, and compatibility maintenance for new OS versions. We provide different maintenance packages to suit your needs and budget.
