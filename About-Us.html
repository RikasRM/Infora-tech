Frequently Asked Questions

What is custom web development?

Custom web development involves creating websites that are tailored specifically to meet the unique needs and objectives of a business. Unlike template-based solutions, custom web development provides greater flexibility, scalability, and the ability to integrate specific functionalities and features.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The development timeline depends on the complexity and scope of the project. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while more complex websites or web applications can take 6-12 weeks or longer. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

What is responsive web design?

Responsive web design is an approach that ensures websites work well on all devices and screen sizes. This means your website will automatically adjust its layout, images, and content to provide an optimal viewing experience whether accessed on a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.

Do you offer website maintenance and support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive website maintenance and support services including regular updates, security monitoring, backup services, performance optimization, and technical support. We offer various maintenance packages to suit different needs and budgets.

What platforms do you use for CMS development?

We work with various CMS platforms including WordPress, Joomla, Drupal, and can also develop custom CMS solutions. We recommend the best platform based on your specific requirements, budget, and technical needs.
